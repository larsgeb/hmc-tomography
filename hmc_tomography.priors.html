Prior objects¶

Prior objects encode prior information on parameters. They are tyically in the form of normalizable distributions (with some exceptions).

Ill-behaved priors¶

Prior distributions which are not normalizable. A Markov chain over these distributions without any other regularization will typically ‘walk away’ to infinity at some point.
